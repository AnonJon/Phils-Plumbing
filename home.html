Let our professional and licensed plumbers keep your water heater in optimal condition. We can advise you about options for repairing or replacing your water heater, keeping customer comfort and cost-efficiency as our top priority. Toilet Repair/Replacement

Clogged toilets happen when items that do not dissolve or break apart in water are flushed down the toilet. These items include feminine hygiene products, baby wipes, paper towels, and dental floss. Our plumbing company is skilled at handling unpleasant toilet clogs. Phil's Plumbing specializes in bathroom plumbing services and will come equipped to handle your toughest problems.
